## Description

In this episode, Matt Wagg of Accelerated Diagnostic &amp; Automotive in Bennington, Nebraska, talks about his automotive journey from the son of a shop owner to opening a shop during COVID and hitting the million-dollar mark three years after putting the keys into the door.
He discusses business plans, the importance of investing in self-education, why you need coaching, and the need for shop owners to have a clear direction. 
Matt taught at the Ratchet+Wrench Management Conference in September
